For Imperial SSH lab machines,
https://www.imperial.ac.uk/computing/people/csg/guides/python/virtual-environment/#d.en.1235829
cd into this directory, and type
export PENV=/vol/bitbucket/${USER}/huaweienv
python3 -m virtualenv $PENV

Then activate it by typing
source $PENV/bin/activate

To deactivate after usage, just type: deactivate
